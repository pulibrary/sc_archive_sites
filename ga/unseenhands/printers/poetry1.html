Center for Research on Women
Stanford University, 1982

Women Writing Poetry in America: Poetry Broadsides by California Women Printers, Palo Alto, California: Matrix Press & The Center for Research on Women, 1982.
Private collection.

The fifteen broadsides in this portfolio were prepared in conjunction with a conference on Women Writing Poetry in America, sponsored by the Center for Research on Women, Stanford University, and held in April, 1982.


Cover by Frances Butler

Brooding Likeness, poem by Louise Glück, art by Wendy Hebborn, printing by Bonnie Bernstein, The Goodmorrow Press. Signed by the author.

Other works in the exhibition:

  • Title page by Kathy Walkup and Connie Thorpe
  • Why Women Need to Dance, illustration: Rock Painting from Spain, poem by Colleen J. McElroy, printing by Callahan and Okamura. Signed by the author.
  • Love Poem, by Audre Lorde, illustration by Patricia Waters, printed at the Calliopea Press by Carol Denison. Signed and numbered 18 of 70 by the author.
  • For Jan, as the End Draws Near, poem by Carolyn Kizer, printed by Kathy Walkup, Matrix Press. Signed by the author.
  • The Good Weef Is Both, poem by Judy Grahn, printed by Cheryl Miller at Interval Press, 1982. Signed by the author.
