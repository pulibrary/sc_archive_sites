Western Americana Manuscripts. Princeton Collections of Western Americana.

One of a deck of 18 playing cards created by Apache Indians in southern Arizona (probably San Carlos Apaches) ca.1870.

See Virginia Wayland, "Princeton Apache Playing Cards," "The Princeton Univeristy Library Chronicle", vol. 34, no. 3 (Spring 1973), pp. 147-157.
