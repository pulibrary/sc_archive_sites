Main Entry: Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
Secondary Entry: A Circular of the High Council
Date : Nauvoo, Illinois, 1846
Material: Broadside, 30 x 23 cm.
"A circular of the High Council to the members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, and to all whom it may concern," announces the departure of the Mormon pioneer company for the Rocky Mountains and enumerates grievances against their enemies in Illinois.
